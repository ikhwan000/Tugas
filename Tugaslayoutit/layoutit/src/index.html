Restaurant

A restaurant or restaurant is a general term for a culinary business, which serves dishes to the public and provides a place to enjoy these dishes and sets certain rates for the food and service. Although restaurants generally serve food on the spot, there are also some that provide food delivery and take-out services as a form of service to their consumers. Restaurants usually specialize in the type of food they serve. For example, Chinese restaurants, Padang restaurants, fast food restaurants and so on.
